# Terms of Service - Implementation Plan

## Overview
Create a comprehensive Terms of Service for the Approach App coaching platform that covers all business aspects including payment processing, data usage, dispute resolution, and regulatory compliance.

## Business Context Analysis
- **Platform**: Dating coaching app with subscription-based revenue model
- **Users**: Dating coaches (service providers) and their clients (18+ only)
- **Payment**: Apple Store In-App Payments
- **Features**: Support Hub, Training Modules, Communication, Journaling, Daily Inspirations
- **Legal Framework**: Existing Privacy Policy and Child Safety Policy
- **Contact**: joe@approach-coach.com (Triumph Joe)

## Terms of Service Structure

### 1. Introduction and Acceptance
- **Acceptance of Terms**: Binding agreement upon app usage
- **Definitions**: Clear definitions of Platform, Services, Users, Coaches, Clients
- **Modifications**: Right to update terms with notice
- **Contact Information**: joe@approach-coach.com

### 2. Eligibility and Account Requirements
- **Age Restriction**: 18+ only (consistent with Child Safety Policy)
- **Account Creation**: Registration requirements and verification
- **Account Security**: User responsibility for credentials
- **Account Termination**: Conditions and procedures

### 3. Platform Services and Usage
- **Service Description**: Coaching platform features and functionality
- **Coach Services**: Training modules, support hub, client management
- **Client Services**: Access to coaching content and communication
- **Acceptable Use**: Guidelines for appropriate platform usage
- **Prohibited Conduct**: Detailed list of forbidden activities

### 4. Subscription and Payment Terms
- **Subscription Plans**: Various coaching service tiers
- **Payment Processing**: Apple Store In-App Payments exclusively
- **Billing Cycles**: Monthly/annual subscription options
- **Auto-Renewal**: Automatic subscription renewal terms
- **Cancellation**: How to cancel subscriptions
- **Refunds**: Refund policy and procedures
- **Price Changes**: Notice requirements for pricing updates

### 5. Content and Intellectual Property
- **User-Generated Content**: Ownership and licensing of user content
- **Coach Content**: Intellectual property rights for coaching materials
- **Platform Content**: Approach App's proprietary content rights
- **Content Guidelines**: Standards for acceptable content
- **DMCA Compliance**: Copyright infringement procedures
- **Content Removal**: Platform's right to remove content

### 6. Privacy and Data Protection
- **Privacy Policy Integration**: Reference to existing Privacy Policy
- **Data Collection**: Types of data collected and usage
- **Communication Monitoring**: Safety and quality assurance
- **Data Sharing**: Limited sharing with service providers
- **User Rights**: Access, deletion, and portability rights

### 7. Coach-Client Relationships
- **Platform Role**: Facilitator, not direct service provider
- **Coach Responsibilities**: Professional conduct and service delivery
- **Client Expectations**: Realistic outcomes and engagement requirements
- **Dispute Resolution**: Between coaches and clients
- **Professional Standards**: Ethical guidelines for coaches
- **Liability Limitations**: Platform's limited responsibility

### 8. Safety and Community Standards
- **Child Safety**: Reference to Child Safety Policy
- **Harassment Prevention**: Zero tolerance for abuse
- **Content Moderation**: Automated and human review processes
- **Reporting Mechanisms**: How to report violations
- **Enforcement Actions**: Warnings, suspensions, terminations

### 9. Third-Party Services and Integrations
- **Apple App Store**: Integration and compliance requirements
- **Analytics Services**: Google Analytics and Firebase
- **Payment Processors**: Apple's payment system
- **Third-Party Liability**: Limitations on platform responsibility
- **Service Availability**: No guarantee of third-party service uptime

### 10. Legal Compliance and Jurisdiction
- **Governing Law**: Applicable legal jurisdiction
- **Regulatory Compliance**: 
  - CCPA/CPRA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  - GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  - CalOPPA (California Online Privacy Protection Act)
  - FOSTA-SESTA compliance
- **Dispute Resolution**: Arbitration and legal procedures
- **Class Action Waiver**: Individual dispute resolution requirement
- **Severability**: Invalid provisions don't void entire agreement

### 11. Limitation of Liability and Disclaimers
- **Service Availability**: No guarantee of uninterrupted service
- **Content Accuracy**: No warranty on user-generated content
- **Coaching Results**: No guarantee of specific outcomes
- **Liability Caps**: Maximum liability limitations

### 12. Termination and Account Closure
- **User Termination**: How users can close accounts
- **Platform Termination**: Conditions for account suspension/closure
- **Data Retention**: Post-termination data handling
- **Survival Clauses**: Terms that continue after termination
- **Final Settlement**: Outstanding obligations resolution

### 13. Updates and Communication
- **Term Updates**: How changes are communicated
- **Service Updates**: Platform improvements and changes

### 14. Contact and Support

- **Legal Notices**: Formal communication procedures
