# Background Video Guide for Approach App Landing Page

## Video Implementation

I've added a subtle background video overlay to your hero section that will play behind your text with 15% opacity, creating a professional and engaging visual experience.

## Video Requirements

### Technical Specifications:
- **Format**: MP4 (best browser compatibility)
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (will loop automatically)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) minimum
- **File Size**: Keep under 5MB for fast loading
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (landscape)

### Content Suggestions:
- **Professional coaching scenarios** (mentor guiding someone)
- **Confident social interactions** (networking events, conversations)
- **Success visualization** (handshakes, positive body language)
- **Urban/modern environments** (coffee shops, co-working spaces, events)
- **Diverse, professional people** in social settings

## How to Add Your Video

1. **Get your video file** (see sources below)
2. **Name it**: `hero-background.mp4`
3. **Place it** in the same folder as your `index.html`
4. **Push to GitHub** - the video will automatically play

## Video Optimization

### Before uploading:
1. **Compress** your video using:
   - HandBrake (free)
   - Adobe Media Encoder
   - Online tools like CloudConvert

2. **Settings for compression**:
   - Bitrate: 1-2 Mbps
   - Frame rate: 24-30 fps
   - Audio: Remove (not needed for background)

## Current Setup

The video will:
